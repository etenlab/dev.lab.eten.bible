ETEN Innovation Lab

The Lab seeks to accelerate Bible translation through advancements in translation resources, quality assurance, artificial intelligence, and crowd sourcing. To facilitate collaboration and reduce rework, the lab is providing a hosted environment for software projects and APIs. If you would like to contribute, submit a proposal, or use the APIs, please email admin@etenlab.com.
